Let's Loan This Loaf

A solution created by Caitlin Becker, Justin Calloway, Brian Lu, and Neha Sharma.

Description

For those who live paycheck to paycheck, a relatively minor emergency can become a catastrophe. Problems that may cost hundreds of dollars to solve balloon into issues that can wreck a family's fiscal future. Payday loans charge exorbitant interest rates in the hopes of trapping people in an endless cycle. Those with poor credit may be turned away by traditional banks, leading many the charge their nearly maxed out credit cards and continuing the cycle of debt. Our solution is to create a collective emergency fund called Let's Loan This Loaf. Instead of large interest rates that loom over you until you pay back the bank, you create a contract with your friend group that details how much everyone contributes and what constitutes a withdrawal. When you get back on your feet, you pay in the loan back in full and no more. This way, friends have an easy, secure way to lend each other money during a crisis, ensure they get their money back, and avoid getting trapped in the cycle of debt.
